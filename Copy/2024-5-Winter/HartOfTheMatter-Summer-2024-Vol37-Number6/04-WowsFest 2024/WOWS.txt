Walk of Western Stars Unveiling and Reception

On November 23rd we celebrated the talents of Martin Kove of Karate Kid and Cobra Kai fame, as well as rodeo-champion and stuntwoman Sammy Thurman-Brackenbury and actress/stunt performer Jeannie Epper. The event was hosted by FOHP President (and Santa Clarita Mayor Pro Tem) Laurene Weste, with help from City Councilwoman Marsha McLean.  Also helping out were FOHP board members Phil Lantis, Bill West, and especially E.J. Stephens who helped organize the event.
