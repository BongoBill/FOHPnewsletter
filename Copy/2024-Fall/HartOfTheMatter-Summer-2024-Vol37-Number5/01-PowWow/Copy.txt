Native American Pow Wow

William S. Hart Regional Park invites the community to enjoy the 30th Annual Hart of the West Pow Wow. Join us as we celebrate Native American Culture, through traditional dance, music, food and craft vendors.  Note that our gift and books stores will also be open during the event.
		     
Saturday, October 5th 10am-6pm & Sunday, October 6th 10am-5pm.

Hosted by the County of Los Angeles Department of Parks and Recreation, The Friends of William S. Hart Park, and the Fernandeno Tataviam Band of Mission Indians.
