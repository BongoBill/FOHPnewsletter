Cowboys and Carols

In honor of William S. Hart's 143 birthday

Saturday, December 8, 2007 will be a special evening
of Bluegrass music and Christmas Carols performed by
the Darrington Family Band. Come tour the mansion and
see the lovely Christmas decorations.  Refreshments will
be served following the performance.  Festivities
begin at 7pm and the cost is $20.00.  Parking is 
located on the lower level and a van will escort guests
to the mansion.
